## Lessons & Gotchas
- react-simple-maps is NOT React 19 compatible. Must use fork: `@vnedyalk0v/react19-simple-maps`
- C-terms (CFR, CIF, CPT, CIP) are the most confusing Incoterms - risk transfers at origin but cost travels to destination. Pipeline visualization is the best way to teach this.
- cytoscape.js adds ~124KB gzip - significant for a learning app. Consider lazy loading if adopted.
